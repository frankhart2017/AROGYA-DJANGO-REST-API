All beans are high in fiber, which is your friend when you're trying to lose weight because it helps you feel fuller longer, thus controlling hunger. Eating beans and legumes has also been linked with various other health benefits, including lowering blood pressure, reducing LDL cholesterol and reducing risk of cardiovascular disease. Beans are fairly low in calories and deliver protein, complex carbohydrates, folate, and iron as well. Beans also have significant amounts of soluble fiber, which is believed to lower blood cholestrol. Eating 100g of beans at a time gives 80 kcal of energy, with 10.5g carbs, 0.5g fat and 9.5g proteins.
